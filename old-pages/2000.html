Information list for representation theory of finite dimensional algebras

- Archive : Meetings 2000 -

  • Darstellungstheorie endlich-dimensionaler Algebren.
    Oberwolfach 20.02. - 26.02.2000. Organizers: Reiten, Ringel.

  • 50th Algebra Day
    Carleton University, Ottawa: April 15, 2000. Speakers: Gabriel, Lusztig, Moody, Zelmanov.

  • British Mathematical Colloquium
    Leeds : April 17 - 20, 2000. Plenary speakers: Atiyah, Donaldson, Friedman, Jantzen, Jones.
    There is a satellite meeting on rings and quantum groups organized by Crawley-Boevey and Jordan.

  • Geometric Methods in Representation Theory
    Bielefeld : April 25 - 28, 2000. Organizers: Ringel, Röhrle

  • Conference Representation Theory and Computational Algebra
    Athens (Georgia): May 15 - 19, 2000. Program Committee: Alperin, Benson, Mason, Milgram, Rickard.
    Principal speakers: Adem, Broué, Cannon.

  • Mid-term workshop of the TMR-network Algebraic Lie Representations
    IHP, Paris : May 22 - 25, 2000. Organizers: Joseph, Rentschler

  • Conference Representations of Algebras and Related Topic
    Northeastern University (Boston): June 3 - 4, 2000. Organizer: Martsinkovsky.

  • Conference: Combinatorics of Lie Type
    Madison (Wisconsin): June 16 - 22, 2000.
    Speakers: Brenti, Broué, Chari, Erdmann, Geck, Ginzburg, Jantzen, Kleshchev, Lehrer, Littelmann, Lusztig, Mathieu, Mathas, Moody, Okounkov, Putcha, Reeder, Shoji, Terao, Thibon, Wachs, Zelevinsky.

  • Perspectives: Discrete Structures in Mathematics, organized by SFB 343
    Bielefeld : June 22 - 24, 2000.

  • ALGEBRA TAG,
    Institut für Experimentelle Mathematik, Essen: June 26, 2000.

  • Workshop: Algebra - Representation Theory (NATO ASI) with Flemish Mornings
    and Euroconference Rings, Modules and Representations
    Constantza: August 2 - 12 and 14 - 18, 2000. Organizers: Marki, van Oystaeyen, Roggenkamp, Stefanescu.

  • ICRA 9
    Beijing: August 21 - September 1, 2000. Organizer: Liu Shao-Xue.

  • Summer School on Geometry of Quiver-representations and Preprojective Algebras,
    Isle of Thorn: September 10 - 17, 2000. Organizers: Erdmann, Crawley-Boevey, Geiß.

  • Algebra and Topology Week, Leicester: September 17 - 23, 2000.

  • Workshop on Computer Algebra and Representation Theory,
    Bielefeld: September 25 - 27, 2000. Organizers: Dräxler, Ringel

  • Workshop on Trends in Noncommutative Algebra and Geometry,
    Bonn, MPI: September 25 - 30, 2000. Organizers: Artin, Bavula, Connes, Manin, Procesi, Van Oystaeyen.
    Sponsor: ESF-programme Noncommutative Geometry

  • Nikolaus conference,
    Aachen, Germany: Dezember 7 - 9, 2000.
    Organizer: Frank Lübeck.

  • Schur Memoriam Workshop,
    Weizmann Institute, Israel: December 27 - 31, 2000.
    Speakers: Andersen, Cherednik, Donkin, Ginzburg, Green, Gorelik, Kazhdan, König, Kostant, Leclerc, Littelmann, Lusztig, Mathieu, Nazarov, Olshanski, Regev, Vasserot, Vershik.
